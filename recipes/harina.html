Harina De Maiz

Description

A sweet cornmeal dish

  • 2 cups Harina De Maiz
  • 5 cups Milk
  • 1 1/2 cups Sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Salt
  1. Mix the salt, sugar, and milk in a pan while over medium
  2. As the mixture comes to a boil add in the Harina while stirring continuously
  3. Continue stirring for 5-10 minutes as the mixture starts to become heavier and comes to a boil
  4. Serve in a bowl and Enjoy!
